Battle Pass

In the current Battle Pass iteration, player progression is measured in two values that correlate with one another. The first is Season Level, which is raised through the accumulation of Season XP Season xp icon.png. The second is Battle Pass Tier, which is represented by the accumulation of Battle Stars Battle star icon.png.

The Battle Pass is a system implemented in Battle Royale to reward players for playing during a pre-established length of time known as a Season. The Battle Pass has its roots in the progression system established in Season 1, although the Battle Pass itself was not formally introduced until Season 2. There are two components to the Battle Pass, one that is freely available to all players and one that is available to purchase in the Item Shop. The latter premium product is actually the Battle Pass itself, with the free component being noted in game as the "Free Pass". Despite this distinction, the Battle Royale community at large frequently uses the term "Battle Pass" to describe both components together along with the current season as a singular entity, e.g. "Battle Pass Season 3" frequently represents all of Season 3 content.

Season level and Season XP

  • Season XP affects Season Level.
  • Season XP is gained by merit: kills in matches, match placement, etc.
  • Season XP can be awarded directly by completing Battle Pass Challenges Battle pass challenge icon.png and by achieving certain tiers in the Battle Pass.
  • Season XP accumulation can be boosted by Personal XP Boosts Personal xp boost icon.png which are rewards in the Battle Pass.
  • Friend XP Boosts Friend xp boost icon.png boost the accumulation of Season XP for those with whom you play (and vice-versa).
  • Combo XP Boosts Combo xp boost icon.pngprovide a boost to both Personal and Friend XP.
